How we met

I first saw Ali in the October, 2011 at a close friend's house while we were attending San Diego State I saw her as soon as I walked in the room. She likes to tease me, but it really was love at first site. From then on I spent time talking to friends and thinking about how to best woo her. After that first night, I invited her on a breakfast date to a small restaurant named The Daily Grind. We dated and got closer for months, with more and more dates, trips to tijuana, and plenty of flowers and surprises. I officially asked her out on Dec. 8, 2012 and we spent the next years getting closer and falling for each other. We traveled together all over the world, from Argentina, to Europe, Costa Rica, and many more. Each trip brought us closer together and ready for the next step in our lives. I know there's only one person that can make me feel as happy and loved as I do and that is Ali. April 21, 2018. 6 years later, I surprised Ali with a trip to Lake Tahoe and asked her to spend the rest of her life with me. I am so honored and happy she said yes! Now we are so excited to share our union with our friends and family!
